Multi-Faceted Interactivity Alignment in Full-Duplex Speech Models

Anonymous Authors

This page presents audio samples for the paper “Multi-Faceted Interactivity Alignment in Full-Duplex Speech Models” (submitted to ARR May 2026).

We apply RL-based post-training to two open-source full-duplex models, Moshi and PersonaPlex, using training data from Fisher and Seamless Interaction. Below, you can listen to and compare the generated audio across base models and their RL-trained variants, evaluated on Full-Duplex-Bench v1 (static, offline evaluation) and Full-Duplex-Bench v2 (multi-turn, real-time interactive evaluation).
